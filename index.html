Places I Worked

Texas Roadhouse was my first job. I started working there when I was 16 and I worked there for 2 years. I started there as a host and got moved to togo when covid started. I took orders and packed togo orders and sometimes made the rolls and salads. I learned a lot while working there and when I quit I was thankful for the experience.

Applebees is where I work at now and I have worked there for a year. It was my first serving job I was scared to start serving at first but I like it better than hosting. Sometimes I get stressed and annoyed but I would never go back to hosting. Applebees always has some promotion going on so we have certain weeks where we get really busy. Right now we have the dollar margaritas and last month we had all you can eat wings. Working in a restaruant there is a lot of drama so I can't wait to get out of the food customer service industry.
